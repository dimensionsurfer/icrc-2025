About the center

Research, funding, outreach, built to move together.

We run an interdisciplinary research center focused on cybersecurity, AI, and digital trust. We fund research through a competitive grants program and operate major outreach platforms, including Cyber Week, AI Week, and DefenseTech Week, that bring together researchers, policymakers, industry leaders, and international partners.

What we do

Interdisciplinary by design

We work across cybersecurity, AI, digital trust, and critical infrastructure, bringing together expertise from computer science, engineering, law, policy, health, and the social sciences.

Research highlights

Visible research outcomes

Three projects capture how the Blavatnik Interdisciplinary Cyber Research Center turned academic work into visible external impact in 2025.

Global security discovery

MadeYouReset

Working with Imperva, researchers from the center uncovered a protocol-level flaw in HTTP/2 affecting more than 100 vendors and potentially billions of users.

Why it matters: we coordinated responsible disclosure through US CERT before the issue could be widely exploited, turning academic discovery into direct public protection.

The December pivot

We kept three flagship platforms in motion.

Blavatnik ICRC kept its global convening role under pressure and delivered the full sequence at scale before year-end.

Cyber Week

A major international convening point

We brought together senior policymakers, researchers, industry leaders, and internationally recognized speakers including Naftali Bennett and Shira Rubinoff to examine cybersecurity strategy, infrastructure resilience, and emerging threats.

AI Week

Research, policy, and public consequence

We connected AI governance, health applications, digital identity, and the institutional consequences of increasingly capable systems in a forum built for researchers, policymakers, and industry decision-makers.

Visit AI Week

DefenseTech Week

Innovation at the intersection of security and technology
